Why a dog? Well, dogs require a lot of attention and time, so someone who is organized, energetic, and responsible can give them what they need. In addition, dogs love having space to run around, so if you like being outside, your canine companion will be right at your side.

Why a cat? Well, cats are somewhat independent animals, meaning that their lives are not bound to yours as tightly, so you can spend less time with them than you do dogs and they will still tolerate you the same. After all, if a creature sleeps sixteen hours a day, it probably won't appreciate being woken up constantly for attention. Cats can be picky eaters, and sometimes they bring food and friends from the outdoors to you as a form of gratitude. If you like fine cuisine, making exotic friends, and can withstand the cold shoulder treatment, you are a cat's perfect host!
