I felt like the search engines gave a lot of jobs that were not necessarily web-related, but that could probably be fixed by playing with the filters a little more. That being said, for these fields, it appears that experience is everything. The best way to get experience is to dive right into something yourself, but an entry-level job would be an excellent way to gain experience. The irony is that many "entry-level" jobs have big requirements, but that sounds like the job market in general. That being said, these results were promising, and I'm sure that much better opportunities can be found with more searching and a willingness to broaden one's knowledge.
